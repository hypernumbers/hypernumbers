Alice needs to collect data from Bob in Sales, Charlie in Engineering and Dave in Ops. She analyses and and graphs the data and she sends it on to Eve who sits on the board.

Collecting the data every week is a pain and she really needs to be sure that the formulae aren't altered so she send copies around.
With Hypernumbers, Alice makes a online team spreadsheet. She creates a page for Bob, Charlie and Dave to put their data and makes it a wiki page with the one-click wiki builder.

Each of them can now only enter data in their own specified cells and Alice can rest assured that no one is going to break her formulae by accident.
Alice gives Eve access to the data and the graphs by making one-click webpages. (Wiki pages and webpages both update in realtime as the spreadsheet recalculates.)

Eve can have a wikipage view of a spreadsheet that Bob sees as a wikipage and that Alice sees as a spreadsheet.
Alice is in control of her spreadsheets. She can use the simple permissions manager to add people to groups.

She can easily build locked-down web-based applications for management information, operational dashboards, consolidated budgetting - whilst keeping her critical business logic safe.
